How To Launch

  1. Collect a Nuclear Keycard from a cargobot, event reward, vendor route, or player source.
  2. Enter Site Alpha, Bravo, or Charlie and fight through to the launch control room.
  3. Insert the Nuclear Keycard, complete launch prep, and enter the matching weekly 8-digit code.
  4. Pick your target on the Appalachian map. The selected silo enters personal cooldown after launch.
  5. Codes rotate every Tuesday at 20:00 UTC. Recheck this page after reset.
